Qard-ul-Hasana

Based on the Islamic concept of Qard-ul-Hasana; Rozgar provides a beneficial loan, which is returned at the end of an agreed period, without any interest, repaid in full as the original amount of the loan.

Qard-ul-Hasana in Light of the Quran & Sunnah

“If you lend Allah a goodly loan, He will double it for you, and will forgive you. And Allah is Most Ready to appreciate and to reward, Most Forbearing.” [Al-Tagabun (64):17]

“ And give Allah a goodly loan.” [ Al-Maidah (5): 12]

“ Those men and women who give Sadaqat and lend Allah a goodly loan, it shall be increased manifold and theirs shall be an honorable good reward.” [ Al-Hadid (57): 18]

“ ...recite as much of the as may be easy for you, and perform As-Salat and give Zakat, and lend to Allah a goodly loan. And whatever good you send before you for yourselves, you will certainly find it with Allah, better and greater in reward. ” [Al-Muzzammil (73): 20]
